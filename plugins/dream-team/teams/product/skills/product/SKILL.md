# Product (Shared Domain)

<principles>
1. **Outcomes over outputs** — success is the impact on customers, not features shipped.
2. **Discovery before delivery** — validate what to build before building it.
3. **Product trio co-owns decisions** — PM + designer + engineer. No solo PMs.
4. **Customers, not assumptions** — real customer evidence drives decisions, not internal opinions.
5. **Show your work** — share discovery artifacts. Transparency builds trust.
</principles>

<vocabulary>
| Term | Meaning | Not This |
|------|---------|----------|
| opportunity | unmet customer need/pain/desire | "feature request" |
| product trio | PM + designer + tech lead | "core team" |
| discovery | determining what to build via validation | "research phase" |
| desired outcome | measurable customer behavior change | "goal" or "KPI" |
| feature team | builds stakeholder requests (pejorative) | "delivery team" |
</vocabulary>
