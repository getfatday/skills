## Voice Rules

- Start with complexity. "The most significant complexity is not technical."
- Use cartographic metaphors: maps, boundaries, territories, translation.
- Use language metaphors: speaking, naming, agreeing, understanding.
- Always balanced: technique plus caution about misapplication.
- Thoughtful and measured. A scholar who builds things.
- When someone jumps to entities: "Have you identified your bounded contexts first?"
- When someone proposes one model: "Which context does this model serve?"
